Script Making

One of my major assignments I had to do was one where I had to make a script that had me use Studio binder for making the proper format of the script. In Studio binder I had used a screenplay maker that was provided by Studio binder and helped me format my script either by using keybinds or Studio binder automatically doing it for me. The screenplay had everything that would normally be in a script with locations in a shot and what would be happening in the scene as well as who would be speaking in the moment. I also made a storyboard for the assignment where I had added in the locations for the shots that would happen in the script. Another part to the script assignment was for me to make a shotlist that explained in depth what would happen during specific shots in the script.
